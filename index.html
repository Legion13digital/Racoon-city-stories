About the game

Resident Evil 2 Remake is a complete redesign of the original 1998 survival horror classic. The game combines tense exploration with brutal combat as you navigate the zombie-infested Raccon City.

Featuring over-the-shoulder camera angels similar to Resident Evil 4, the game offers a modernized control scheme while maintaining the classic horror atmosphere that made the original so beloved.

With photorealistic visuals, heart-pounding audio, and new gameplay mechanics, this is the definitive way to experience the nightmare of Racoon City.

Main Characters

Survivors of the Outbreak

Play as rookie police officer Leon S. Kennedy and college student Claire Redfield, who are thrust info a nightmare when they arrive in Racoon City amidst a devastating zombie outbreak.

Each characters has their own separate campaign with unique story elements, items, and areas to explore. Their paths cross throughout the game, but their experiences are distinct.

Others characters include the mysterious Ada Wong and the your Shelly Birkin, each with their own mitivations and roles in the unfolding tragedy.

Gameplay Features

Survival Horror Perfected

Scavenge for limited resources while solving puzzles and fighting terrifying enemies in a desperate fight for survival.

The game features the iconic inventory management system, where every bullet and herb counts. Choose whether to fight or flee from the horrors that stalk the corridors of Racoon City.

Experience the relentless pursuit of Mr. X, an unstoppable tyrant that will hunt you throughout the game, creating, constant tension and fear.
